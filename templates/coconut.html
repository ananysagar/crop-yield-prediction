Coconut Diseases & Symptoms

Important Information

This guide helps you identify common coconut diseases and their symptoms for better crop management.

Bud Rot

  • ✓ Yellowing of young leaves
  • ✓ Rotting of spear leaf
  • ✓ Crown wilting

Treatment

Remove affected parts and apply Bordeaux mixture to the crown.

Stem Bleeding

  • ✓ Dark brown liquid oozing from trunk
  • ✓ Reduced leaf size
  • ✓ Yellowing of fronds

Treatment

Clean affected areas and apply fungicide paste.

Root Wilt

  • ✓ Yellowing of older leaves
  • ✓ Flaccidity of leaflets
  • ✓ Reduced nut size

Treatment

Improve drainage and apply recommended fertilizers.

Leaf Rot

  • ✓ Rotting of mature leaves
  • ✓ Brown lesions on leaflets
  • ✓ Premature nut fall

Treatment

Remove affected leaves and spray with fungicides.

Prevention Tips

  • Regular monitoring of trees
  • Proper drainage system
  • Regular cleaning of crown
  • Balanced fertilization
  • Proper spacing between trees
  • Regular pest control measures
