Ginger Diseases & Symptoms

Important Information

This guide helps you identify common ginger diseases and their symptoms for better crop management.

Soft Rot

  • ✓ Water-soaked lesions on rhizomes
  • ✓ Yellowing and wilting of leaves
  • ✓ Collapsing of pseudostems

Treatment

Remove infected plants, improve drainage, and apply copper-based fungicides.

Bacterial Wilt

  • ✓ Stunted growth
  • ✓ Leaf yellowing and wilting
  • ✓ Dark discoloration of rhizomes

Treatment

Use disease-free planting material and practice crop rotation.

Leaf Spot

  • ✓ Small, oval spots on leaves
  • ✓ Brown lesions with yellow halos
  • ✓ Premature leaf fall

Treatment

Apply appropriate fungicides and maintain proper plant spacing.

Rhizome Rot

  • ✓ Rotting of rhizomes
  • ✓ Yellowing of shoots
  • ✓ Plant death

Treatment

Treat seed rhizomes with fungicides and ensure proper soil drainage.

Prevention Tips

  • Use disease-free planting material
  • Practice crop rotation
  • Maintain proper drainage
  • Regular field monitoring
  • Proper plant spacing
  • Balanced fertilization
