Cashew Diseases & Symptoms

Important Information

This guide helps you identify common cashew diseases and their symptoms for better crop management.

Anthracnose

  • ✓ Dark brown lesions
  • ✓ Leaf and flower damage
  • ✓ Fruit rot

Treatment

Apply copper-based fungicides and prune affected parts.

Dieback Disease

  • ✓ Wilting of shoots
  • ✓ Branch death
  • ✓ Gum exudation

Treatment

Remove affected branches and apply fungicides.

Powdery Mildew

  • ✓ White powdery coating
  • ✓ Leaf curling
  • ✓ Flower drop

Treatment

Apply sulfur-based fungicides and ensure good air circulation.

Root Rot

  • ✓ Yellowing of leaves
  • ✓ Root decay
  • ✓ Tree decline

Treatment

Improve drainage and apply appropriate fungicides.

Prevention Tips

  • Regular pruning
  • Proper spacing
  • Good drainage
  • Clean cultivation
  • Balanced fertilization
  • Disease monitoring
