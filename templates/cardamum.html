Cardamum Diseases & Symptoms

Important Information

This guide helps you identify common cardamum diseases and their symptoms for better crop management.

Capsule Rot

  • ✓ Water-soaked lesions on capsules
  • ✓ Dark brown to black spots
  • ✓ Premature capsule drop

Treatment

Apply copper oxychloride and improve drainage.

Leaf Blight

  • ✓ Oval to elongated spots on leaves
  • ✓ Yellowing of leaf margins
  • ✓ Leaf wilting

Treatment

Remove infected leaves and spray fungicides.

Rhizome Rot

  • ✓ Yellowing of leaves
  • ✓ Root decay
  • ✓ Plant collapse

Treatment

Remove affected plants and treat soil with fungicides.

Mosaic Disease

  • ✓ Mottled appearance on leaves
  • ✓ Stunted growth
  • ✓ Reduced yield

Treatment

Remove infected plants and control insect vectors.

Prevention Tips

  • Proper shade management
  • Regular monitoring
  • Good drainage system
  • Clean cultivation
  • Disease-free planting material
  • Balanced fertilization
