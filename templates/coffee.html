Coffee Diseases & Symptoms

Important Information

This guide helps you identify common coffee diseases and their symptoms for better crop management.

Coffee Leaf Rust

  • ✓ Orange-yellow spots on leaves
  • ✓ Premature leaf drop
  • ✓ Reduced yield

Treatment

Apply copper-based fungicides and maintain proper plant spacing.

Coffee Berry Disease

  • ✓ Dark sunken lesions on berries
  • ✓ Premature berry drop
  • ✓ Mummified berries

Treatment

Regular fungicide application and proper pruning.

Brown Eye Spot

  • ✓ Circular brown spots with light centers
  • ✓ Leaf yellowing
  • ✓ Defoliation

Treatment

Improve air circulation and apply appropriate fungicides.

Root Rot

  • ✓ Wilting of leaves
  • ✓ Yellowing of foliage
  • ✓ Stunted growth

Treatment

Improve drainage and apply fungicides to soil.

Prevention Tips

  • Regular monitoring of plants
  • Proper irrigation management
  • Adequate plant spacing
  • Balanced fertilization
  • Regular pruning
  • Weed control
