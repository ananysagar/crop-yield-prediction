Cocoa Diseases & Management

Important Information

Learn about common cocoa diseases, their symptoms, and effective management strategies for better yield.

Black Pod Disease

  • ✓ Brown lesions on pods
  • ✓ White fungal growth
  • ✓ Pod rot and mummification

Treatment

Apply copper-based fungicides and remove infected pods. Maintain proper drainage.

Witches' Broom

  • ✓ Abnormal shoot growth
  • ✓ Malformed pods
  • ✓ Hardened green pods

Treatment

Remove infected parts and maintain proper pruning. Use resistant varieties.

Vascular Streak Dieback

  • ✓ Leaf chlorosis
  • ✓ Branch dieback
  • ✓ Spotted leaf scars

Treatment

Remove infected branches and apply fungicides. Use disease-free seedlings.

Stem Canker

  • ✓ Bark lesions
  • ✓ Gum exudation
  • ✓ Stem dieback

Treatment

Clean affected areas and apply fungicide paste. Improve tree vigor.

Prevention Tips

  • Regular pruning and sanitation
  • Proper shade management
  • Balanced fertilization
  • Good air circulation
  • Proper spacing between trees
  • Regular monitoring for early detection
