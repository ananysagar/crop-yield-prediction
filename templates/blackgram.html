Blackgram Diseases & Symptoms

Important Information

This guide helps you identify common blackgram diseases and their symptoms for better crop management.

Yellow Mosaic Disease

  • ✓ Yellow and green mottling
  • ✓ Stunted growth
  • ✓ Reduced pod formation

Treatment

Control whitefly vectors and use resistant varieties.

Powdery Mildew

  • ✓ White powdery growth
  • ✓ Leaf distortion
  • ✓ Premature defoliation

Treatment

Apply sulfur-based fungicides and ensure proper spacing.

Root Rot

  • ✓ Rotting of roots
  • ✓ Wilting of plants
  • ✓ Yellowing of leaves

Treatment

Improve drainage and use seed treatment with fungicides.

Leaf Spot

  • ✓ Brown circular spots
  • ✓ Leaf yellowing
  • ✓ Defoliation

Treatment

Apply copper-based fungicides and practice crop rotation.

Prevention Tips

  • Use disease-free seeds
  • Proper field drainage
  • Crop rotation
  • Timely sowing
  • Vector control
  • Regular monitoring
