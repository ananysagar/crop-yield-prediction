Pepper Diseases & Management

Important Information

Learn about common pepper diseases, their symptoms, and effective management strategies for better yield.

Quick Wilt Disease

  • ✓ Sudden wilting of leaves and branches
  • ✓ Yellowing of leaves
  • ✓ Root and collar rot

Treatment

Apply Bordeaux mixture and maintain proper drainage. Remove affected vines.

Pollu Disease

  • ✓ Black spots on developing berries
  • ✓ Premature berry drop
  • ✓ Internal decay of berries

Treatment

Spray copper oxychloride or Bordeaux mixture. Remove infected berries.

Slow Decline

  • ✓ Gradual yellowing of leaves
  • ✓ Defoliation and die-back
  • ✓ Root decay

Treatment

Apply nematicides and maintain soil health. Practice crop rotation.

Anthracnose

  • ✓ Small, circular spots on leaves
  • ✓ Sunken lesions on berries
  • ✓ Premature leaf fall

Treatment

Apply fungicides and ensure proper spacing between vines.

Prevention Tips

  • Use disease-free planting material
  • Maintain proper drainage
  • Regular pruning and training
  • Balanced fertilization
  • Proper spacing between plants
  • Regular monitoring for early detection
