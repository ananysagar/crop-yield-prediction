Paddy Diseases & Management

Important Information

Learn about common paddy diseases, their symptoms, and effective management strategies for better yield.

Blast Disease

  • ✓ Diamond-shaped lesions on leaves
  • ✓ Dark brown spots with gray centers
  • ✓ Affected nodes turn blackish

Treatment

Apply fungicides like Tricyclazole or Isoprothiolane. Use resistant varieties.

Bacterial Leaf Blight

  • ✓ Yellow to white lesions along leaf margins
  • ✓ Lesions turn straw-colored
  • ✓ Leaves dry up and die

Treatment

Use copper-based bactericides and maintain proper field drainage.

Brown Spot

  • ✓ Oval or circular brown spots
  • ✓ Gray centers in spots
  • ✓ Reduced grain quality

Treatment

Apply proper nutrients and use fungicides like Mancozeb.

Sheath Blight

  • ✓ Irregular lesions on leaf sheaths
  • ✓ Greenish-gray water-soaked spots
  • ✓ Lodging of plants

Treatment

Use fungicides like Hexaconazole and maintain optimal spacing.

Prevention Tips

  • Use disease-resistant varieties
  • Maintain proper field sanitation
  • Follow balanced fertilization
  • Proper water management
  • Timely planting and harvesting
  • Regular field monitoring
