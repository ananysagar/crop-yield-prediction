Tea Diseases & Symptoms

Important Information

This guide helps you identify common tea diseases and their symptoms for better crop management.

Blister Blight

  • ✓ Translucent spots on young leaves
  • ✓ Brown blisters on leaf surface
  • ✓ Defoliation in severe cases

Treatment

Apply copper-based fungicides and maintain proper air circulation.

Black Rot

  • ✓ Dark brown to black lesions
  • ✓ Wilting of leaves
  • ✓ Branch dieback

Treatment

Remove infected parts and apply systemic fungicides.

Red Rust

  • ✓ Orange-red spots on leaves
  • ✓ Premature leaf fall
  • ✓ Reduced plant vigor

Treatment

Apply appropriate fungicides and improve soil drainage.

Root Rot

  • ✓ Yellowing of leaves
  • ✓ Stunted growth
  • ✓ Root decay

Treatment

Improve soil drainage and apply fungicides to the soil.

Prevention Tips

  • Proper pruning and spacing
  • Regular monitoring
  • Good drainage system
  • Balanced fertilization
  • Weed control
  • Proper shade management
