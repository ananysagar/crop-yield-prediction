Groundnut Diseases & Symptoms

Important Information

This guide helps you identify common groundnut diseases and their symptoms for better crop management.

Early Leaf Spot

  • ✓ Small brown spots with yellow halos
  • ✓ Spots on upper leaf surface
  • ✓ Premature defoliation

Treatment

Apply fungicides and maintain proper spacing between plants.

Late Leaf Spot

  • ✓ Dark brown to black spots
  • ✓ Spots on lower leaf surface
  • ✓ Severe defoliation

Treatment

Use resistant varieties and apply appropriate fungicides.

Rust Disease

  • ✓ Orange-red pustules
  • ✓ Yellowing of leaves
  • ✓ Reduced pod formation

Treatment

Apply sulfur-based fungicides and practice crop rotation.

Collar Rot

  • ✓ Rotting at soil level
  • ✓ Wilting of plants
  • ✓ Plant death

Treatment

Improve drainage and treat seeds with fungicides.

Prevention Tips

  • Crop rotation
  • Proper field drainage
  • Resistant varieties
  • Timely sowing
  • Balanced fertilization
  • Regular monitoring
