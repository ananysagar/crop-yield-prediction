Arecanut Diseases & Symptoms

Important Information

Learn about common arecanut diseases, their symptoms, and treatment methods for better crop management.

Fruit Rot / Koleroga

  • ✓ Nuts turn black and soft
  • ✓ White fungal growth on nuts
  • ✓ Premature nut fall

Treatment

Apply Bordeaux mixture before monsoon and spray fungicides.

Yellow Leaf Disease

  • ✓ Yellowing of leaves
  • ✓ Reduced nut size
  • ✓ Stunted growth

Treatment

Apply balanced fertilizers and maintain proper irrigation.

Bud Rot

  • ✓ Rotting of terminal bud
  • ✓ Yellowing of crown leaves
  • ✓ Crown wilting

Treatment

Remove affected parts and apply fungicide paste.

Stem Bleeding

  • ✓ Dark patches on stem
  • ✓ Reddish brown liquid oozing
  • ✓ Decay of affected tissues

Treatment

Clean affected areas and apply Bordeaux paste.

Prevention Tips

  • Maintain proper drainage
  • Regular inspection of palms
  • Timely removal of affected parts
  • Proper spacing between palms
  • Regular application of fertilizers
  • Prophylactic spraying before monsoon
